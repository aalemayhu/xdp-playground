Welcome to the XDP playground!

This is a open list challenge which if you follow will teach you everything
you ever wanted to know about XDP. To complete the challenge you have to
write real programs. Your programs gets automatically verified so zero
waiting (famous last words).

To start the challenge use the picker located somewhere on the page. This
is not a speed contest. If it takes longer than you'd like, take a break
or go read the man pages and make sure you understand it.

Enjoy your to journey into the world of high speed packet mangling.
